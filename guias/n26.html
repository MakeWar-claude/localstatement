How to download your N26 statement

Steps to get the official PDF statement from N26's app or website, based on the bank's official help. Once downloaded, convert it to Excel without uploading it anywhere.

Steps

  1. Open the N26 app and go to the Home tab.
  2. Choose the account or Space you want, then select Downloads.
  3. Under Bank statements, pick the month you need.
  4. Choose PDF (CSV also available) and tap Download.

Good to know

N26 statements are official documents, valid without signature or stamp. Both the monthly statements and the custom-range activity reports convert cleanly in LocalStatement.
